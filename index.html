Beerpop: The Ultimate Guide to Beer Trends

Ever wondered why your favorite beer feels like poetry in a pint? In this project, we dive into the frothy depths of beer preferences, ratings, and trends across the globe.

Our mission: to uncover the science of what makes a beer legendary. From understanding seasonal trends to exploring cultural influences, we’ve set out to answer one big question: what makes a beer unforgettable?

Meet the Data

Our analysis is based on data from BeerAdvocate and RateBeer. These platforms host an ocean of beer reviews and ratings from around the world. But don’t worry, we’ve sifted through the froth to make sense of it all.

Key definitions:

  • Beer Preference: The specific features that people love in their beers—be it fruity notes, malty sweetness, or even just a catchy name.
  • Popularity: The combination of high ratings and wide recognition in the beer community.
