Beerpop: The Ultimate Guide to Beer Trends

Have you ever paused mid-sip and wondered what truly makes a beer legendary? Is it the boldness of its hops, the warmth of its alcohol content, or perhaps the lyrical reviews of enthusiasts who compare it to liquid gold? Welcome to Hop, Skip, and Analyze, where we dive into the frothy world of beer to unravel the mysteries of its popularity. Using data from BeerAdvocate, the go-to haven for beer connoisseurs, we’re blending numbers and words to explore how factors like style, alcohol content, and review sentiment drive a beer’s success.

But we’re not stopping there! From the bustling pubs of the UK to the Oktoberfest-loving heart of Germany and the craft breweries of the US, we’re mapping how beer trends ebb and flow across regions and seasons. Are high-alcohol beers the winter MVPs? Do citrusy ales dominate the summer? And why does one IPA make waves while another sinks? With data science tools in hand and curiosity on tap, we’re chasing the ultimate question: what makes a beer unforgettable?

Take your favourite beer, sit back and enjoy the ride. We promise you won't be disappointed.
